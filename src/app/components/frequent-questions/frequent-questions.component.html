    What is the store's schedule and when can I order?

    The store's schedule is from 8:00 to 17:00 from Monday to Friday, but orders can be placed at any time, those past 5 pm will be taken the next day and those on Saturday and Sunday, only on Mondays.

    I received a different product than the one I ordered. What to do?

    If you received a product other than the one ordered, we recommend that you refuse the package upon delivery. If you have accepted the package, please contact us via the phone number available on the site.
